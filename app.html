About this app

StudentCC is a comprehensive Android application designed specifically for VIT students to simplify their academic life. It integrates multiple essential services into one convenient platform, making student life more organized and efficient.

Key Features

  • VTOP Integration: Access VTOP services directly from the app
  • Attendance Tracking: Monitor your attendance percentage in real-time
  • CGPA Calculator: Calculate and track your CGPA with detailed breakdown
  • Schedule Management: View and manage your class schedule
  • Notifications: Get timely reminders for classes and deadlines
  • Offline Support: Access basic features without internet
  • Dark Mode: Beautiful dark theme for better viewing
  • Biometric Security: Secure login with fingerprint
